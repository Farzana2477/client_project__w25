First Gen at UMSI

About Us

We are a welcoming community of First Generation Students who are empowered to be successful and support our peers to be successful. We believe that with teamwork and guidance on the resources UM has to provide, we can all achieve our dreams.

Am I a first-generation college student?

At the University of Michigan, undergraduates and graduate students are considered first-generation college students if neither parent or guardian has completed a 4-year college or university degree.
